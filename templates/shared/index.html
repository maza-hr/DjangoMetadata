Site for Metadata


This website is a prototype being developed to manage data-related information. It encompasses the structure of the pipeline, representing the flow of data processing from collection to making it available to development teams.

Its primary goal is to record and oversee all data manipulations, highlighting transformation and organization stages, and any modifications made before they are used by development teams in their projects.

As a secondary objective, it serves as a laboratory to experiment with open-source tools for metadata registration, manipulation, and storage routines, aiming to expand these practices to other departments.
